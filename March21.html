Old-Site of the Day: Open DOS Unofficial Home Page It's 1997. Microsoft has locked out most other OS makers from the market. The rights to DR-DOS, Digital Research's upgraded, MS-DOS compatible version of CP/M-86 have passed through several different hands. Finally the last owner, Caldera, has decided to call it quits, & they anounce that they're going to release the OS as an open source program, OpenDOS. This page is a fan site for the OS, made just after the announcement, but before the OS was actually open-sourced. I'm not sure if it ever was; sometimes things don't work out the way they're planned.
