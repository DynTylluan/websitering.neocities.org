Web-Site-Ring's Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. As such, we neither collect nor store any personally identifiable information; not screen names, not email addresses. Not even IP addresses.

Cookies are small pieces of data websites store on your computer. They were first used to provide personalization, but they can be used to track users. We don't use cookies either.

We make use of other online services, which have their own policies. Some of these services include:

  • Neocities, our web-host.
  • Google, for some video hosting.
  • Wikimedia, for some image hosting.
  • SiteLevel, runs our site search engine.
  • SonicRun, another search engine. I added their search box to a page as an example of what they offer.
  • xkcd, I've embeded a few comics I thought were interesting.
  • Serebii.net Hotlinked to a few of his buttons.
  • The Mushroom Kingdom Hotlinked to a few more< buttons.

We use a few other services as well. Some of services we use collect information. If you want to review their privacy policies, follow a link to their sites.

We do provide links to hundreds of sites; we cannot be responsible for the content of sites we link to.
